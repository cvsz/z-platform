# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

Participants in `z-platform` are expected to collaborate professionally, respectfully, and in good faith. The project depends on careful review, clear communication, and a shared commitment to secure platform engineering.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while still reviewing rigorously.
- Keep discussions focused on the work, evidence, and safety boundaries.
- Welcome questions from new contributors.
- Report security issues privately instead of exposing exploitable details publicly.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information or credentials.
- Bypassing security, approval, billing, or production-readiness rules.
- Pressuring contributors to skip review or safety checks.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, restrict repository access, or escalate to organization owners when behavior conflicts with this code of conduct.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to the repository owner or maintainer team. Security vulnerabilities should follow `SECURITY.md`.
